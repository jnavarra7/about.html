    ABOUT SC ARCHIVES
    SC ARCHIVES was created with the purpose of making affordable USC merch accessible for anyone to buy. Through just a simple google form, any student, staff , or alumni can submit lightly used or well cared USC items including clothing, textbooks, football tickets, etc. The goal of SC ARCHIVES is to allow less waste from people who no longer are in need of their items and offer cheaper alternatives for people who might not want to pay full price at the USC Bookstore.
